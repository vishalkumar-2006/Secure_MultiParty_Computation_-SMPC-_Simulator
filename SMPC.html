Features & Design Goals

Designed with the 8 Golden Rules
  • Consistency across UI, colors, and terminology
  • Shortcuts and expert-friendly controls
  • Informative, numeric feedback for each step
  • Clear closure (completion modal and summary)
Simulation Capabilities
  • Additive sharing modulo a prime (random shares)
  • Shamir sharing with polynomial generation and Lagrange reconstruction (numeric)
  • Step-by-step view of Lagrange terms and running sums
  • Error prevention & contextual validation
